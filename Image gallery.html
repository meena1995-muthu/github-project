Cat


The cat, also called domestic cat and house cat, is a small carnivorous mammal.

Dog


The dog is a domesticated descendant of wolves. Also called the domestic dog.

Rabbit


Rabbits, or bunnies, are small mammals in the family Leporidae, which is in the order Lagomorpha.

Monkey


Monkey is a common name that may refer to most mammals of the infraorder Simiiformes, also known as simians.

Goat


The goat or domestic goat (Capra hircus) is a species of goat-antelope that is mostly kept as livestock.

Cow


Cows are the most common domesticated animal in the world. There are over 1,000 different breeds of cows and they are found everywhere except Antarctica.

Sheep


Sheep or domestic sheep are a domesticated, ruminant mammal typically kept as livestock.

Horse


The horse is a domesticated, one-toed, hoofed mammal. It belongs to the taxonomic family Equidae.

Pig


The pig, also called swine or hog, is an omnivorous, domesticated, even-toed, hoofed mammal.
